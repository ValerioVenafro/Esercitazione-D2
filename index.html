Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberriers, and Brie

wow your taste buds whith this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberriers, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

for the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan
For the Topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though, it's not necessary
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sizer mixing bowl, or in a stand mixed with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorder. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
